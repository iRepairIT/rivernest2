Common Questions

Portal essentials.

When is rent due?

Rent is due on the 1st of each month. A grace period through the 5th is provided. After the 5th, late fees apply per the terms of your lease. You can set up auto-pay through the portal to avoid late charges.

How do I submit a maintenance request?

Log in to the portal and select "Maintenance" from the menu. Describe the issue, attach photos if helpful, and submit. You'll receive an SMS or email confirmation, and we'll follow up within one business day to schedule service.

What if I have an emergency after hours?

For genuine emergencies — water leaks, no heat in winter, no AC in extreme heat, electrical issues, lockouts — call our emergency line at (678) 486-6364 and follow the prompts. For all other issues, please use the portal.

Can I view a copy of my lease?

Yes. Once logged in, your full lease and any addenda are available under "Documents." You can download a PDF copy at any time.

How do I update my contact information?

Inside the portal, go to "Account" then "Profile" to update your phone, email, and emergency contacts. Keeping this information current helps us reach you about important property matters.
