About River Nest

A small team that knows every home we manage.

River Nest Properties is a residential rental management company based in Smyrna, Georgia. We focus on a curated portfolio of homes throughout the Atlanta metro — single-family residences, townhomes, and small-property rentals where the details actually matter.

Our approach is simple: keep homes well-maintained, keep tenants informed, and keep communication direct. No automated runarounds, no shifting points of contact.
