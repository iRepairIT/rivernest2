Privacy Policy

How River Nest Properties collects, uses, and protects your information — and how SMS communications work.

Last updated: April 27, 2026

River Nest Properties ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect personal information when you visit our website, contact us, apply for a rental, or use our tenant services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact information — name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Rental application information — employment, income verification, rental history, references, and other information required to evaluate applications
  • Tenant account information — payment history, maintenance requests, lease documents, and communications with our team
  • Website usage information — IP address, browser type, pages visited, and similar technical data collected automatically

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide information about available rentals
  • Process rental applications and verify applicant information
  • Manage tenant accounts, lease agreements, and rent payments
  • Coordinate maintenance and property service requests
  • Send important account, leasing, and property notifications
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements

3. SMS Communications & Opt-In

By providing your mobile phone number and checking the SMS consent box on any of our forms, you opt in to receive text message (SMS) communications from River Nest Properties.

Types of messages you may receive

  • Leasing updates — application status, showing confirmations, lease document notices
  • Maintenance communication — request confirmations, technician scheduling, service updates
  • Account notifications — rent due reminders, payment confirmations, important account alerts

Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on your interactions with us. You may receive messages occasionally (a few per month) or more frequently during active leasing or maintenance situations.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile carrier and plan. River Nest Properties does not charge for the SMS messages we send.

Opting out

You can opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. After replying STOP, you will receive a final confirmation message and no further texts will be sent. To resume messages later, reply START.

Help

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, or contact us directly at (678) 486-6364 or info@rivernestproperties.com.

Sample SMS messages

Below are examples of the types of text messages River Nest Properties may send:

River Nest Properties: Your rent payment is due tomorrow. Reply STOP to opt out.
River Nest Properties: Your maintenance request has been received. We will update you shortly. Reply STOP to opt out.
River Nest Properties: A new rental listing is available. Contact us for details. Reply STOP to opt out.

Phone number privacy

We do not share, sell, rent, or transfer phone numbers or SMS opt-in data to any third parties for marketing purposes. Phone numbers and SMS consent information are used solely to communicate with you on behalf of River Nest Properties.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — companies that help us operate our business (e.g., payment processors, maintenance contractors, background-check providers) under confidentiality obligations
  • Property owners — to the extent necessary to manage the property you rent
  • Legal compliance — when required by law, court order, or to protect our rights or the safety of others

5. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. While no system is perfectly secure, we work to maintain industry-standard protections for your data.

6. Cookies & Website Analytics

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences and understand how visitors use our site. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality.

7. Your Choices

You may:

  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Opt out of SMS communications by replying STOP
  • Unsubscribe from email communications using the link in any marketing email
  • Request that we delete information we no longer need to retain

To make a request, contact us at info@rivernestproperties.com or (678) 486-6364.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes will be communicated through our website or by direct notice where appropriate.
